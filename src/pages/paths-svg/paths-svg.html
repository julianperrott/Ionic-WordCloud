SVG Paths

The "curve" method sets the curve factory and returns the line generator. Instead of just providing the data to the svg container, we provide the generated data by the chosen generator.

  • d3Shape.curveCardinalOpen
  • d3Shape.curveCatmullRom
  • d3Shape.curveCatmullRomClosed
  • d3Shape.curveCatmullRomOpen
  • d3Shape.curveLinear
  • d3Shape.curveLinearClosed
  • d3Shape.curveMonotoneX
  • d3Shape.curveMonotoneY
  • d3Shape.curveNatural
